Terms of Service

Last updated: June 2026

These terms govern your use of Bridge Broker ("we," "us," "the platform"). By posting a listing, browsing, or contacting a seller through us, you agree to them. We're an early-stage platform, not yet a registered company — these terms will be formalized as we grow.

1. What we are

Bridge Broker is a vehicle marketplace and brokerage for cars, motorcycles, and bajajs in Ethiopia. We review and publish listings, route buyer inquiries through our own broker contact details, and help connect buyers and sellers. We are not the owner of any vehicle listed, and we are not a party to the sale itself unless explicitly stated (e.g. when escrow is used).

2. Posting a listing

  • Posting is free. Submitting a listing does not guarantee approval — we review every listing before it goes live.
  • You must own the vehicle or have authorization to sell it, and the information you provide (condition, price, photos) must be accurate.
  • Once approved, your listed phone number is replaced with our broker number — buyers will contact us, not you directly, unless we connect you for a viewing.

3. Commission

  • We charge a commission, calculated as a percentage of the final sale price, on vehicles sold through the platform.
  • The commission rate is set per listing and communicated to you before your listing is approved and published — you will know the rate in advance, not after a sale.
  • For sales completed off-platform (the typical case — buyer and seller meet directly for the handoff), the commission is collected separately and is your responsibility to pay once the sale is confirmed.
  • For sales completed using our optional escrow service, the commission is deducted automatically from the buyer's payment before the remaining amount is paid out to you.

4. Featured Listings

Featured Listings is an optional paid upgrade for additional visibility, billed at the rate shown on our Pricing page at the time of purchase, processed through Chapa. Featured status expires automatically at the end of the paid period and is non-refundable once activated, except where required by law.

5. Escrow (optional buyer protection)

  • Most sales are not escrowed — the default is a direct in-person handoff between buyer and seller, with no platform money involved.
  • Escrow is only used when a buyer specifically requests it, and is set up by an admin for a specific listing.
  • When escrow is used, the buyer's payment is held until the admin confirms the vehicle handoff occurred, at which point funds (minus commission) are released to the seller, or refunded to the buyer if the sale doesn't go through.
  • Payout to the seller after release is currently handled manually by the admin (bank or mobile money transfer) — it is not instant.
  • If a dispute is raised, the admin reviews it before releasing or refunding funds. We aim to be fair to both sides but our decision on disputed escrow transactions is final.

6. Verification

The Verified Broker Badge reflects that an admin has reviewed a seller's submitted ID. It is a check we perform in good faith, not a guarantee against fraud, and does not make us liable for the actions of a verified seller.

7. Our role and limitations

  • We review listings for completeness and obvious red flags, but we cannot independently verify every claim a seller makes about a vehicle's condition or history.
  • We are not responsible for the condition of any vehicle sold through the platform, and we encourage every buyer to inspect a vehicle in person before completing a purchase.
  • Outside of held escrow funds, we do not guarantee any transaction and are not a party to the underlying sale contract between buyer and seller.

8. Prohibited use

  • No fraudulent, stolen, or misrepresented vehicles.
  • No attempting to bypass our broker contact details to avoid commission on a sale that originated through the platform.
  • No harassment of other users, admins, or buyers/sellers contacted through the platform.

We reserve the right to remove a listing, suspend an account, or decline to publish a listing at our discretion, particularly where these terms are violated.

9. Changes to these terms

We may update these terms as the platform evolves. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated terms. Material changes (e.g. to commission structure) will be reflected on our Pricing page as well.

This is a working draft, not reviewed by a lawyer, and does not constitute legal advice. If you have concerns about a specific transaction, please contact us directly.
