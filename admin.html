📋 All Listings

Most sales happen off-platform — buyer and seller meet directly. When a listing sells, click Mark as Sold to record the final price; commission owed is calculated from the rate you set when approving it. If the sale went through Escrow instead, it's already marked sold automatically and the commission was deducted from the payout — no need to do it again here.

Once a listing is sold, you can also Delete Photos to free up storage — this only removes the images; the sale record, price, and commission info stay intact.

🔒 Escrow Transactions

Most sales still happen the normal way — buyer and seller meet and hand off directly. Use this only when a buyer asks for protection: they pay the full price into escrow, it shows as held here, and you release it to the seller (or refund it) once the handoff is confirmed.

✅ Payout is automatic: When you click Release & Pay, your commission is deducted and the seller's cut is immediately queued for bank transfer via Chapa. The seller must have saved their bank details in Seller Dashboard → Bank Details first — if they haven't, you'll see a "Pay Seller" button to retry once they do.

⚙️ Commission Settings

These are just the defaults pre-filled in the Approve Listing modal — every listing's actual rate is still set (and can be overridden) at approval time, and a sale's commission_owed is locked in at the moment it's marked sold, so changing these later never affects past sales.

Saved to this browser only (localStorage), since there's no settings table in the database — if you approve listings from a different device/browser, set these there too.

💲 Featured Listing Prices

Set the prices sellers pay to feature their listing. Changes take effect immediately — the next seller who opens the Feature panel will see the new prices. Saved to Supabase so all devices stay in sync.
