Featured Listings

Get more eyes on your vehicle with priority placement.

By default, every approved listing appears in normal search order. Featured Listings is an optional paid upgrade that moves your vehicle to the top of the results and adds a visible ⭐ badge, so buyers see it first.

What you get

  • Priority placement — Featured listings are sorted above standard ones across the homepage and listings page.
  • ⭐ Featured badge — shown directly on your listing card so it stands out at a glance.
  • Runs automatically — once your time period ends, your listing reverts to standard placement on its own. No need to remember to cancel anything.

How to upgrade a listing

  1. Log in and open your Seller Dashboard.
  2. Go to the Featured Listings section in the sidebar.
  3. Choose which of your approved listings to feature, pick a plan, and pay securely via Chapa.
  4. Once payment is confirmed, the ⭐ badge and priority placement apply automatically — no admin action needed.

Featured status only applies to listings that are already approved and live. If your listing is still pending review, get it approved first, then feature it from your dashboard.

A note on payments

Featured payments are processed through Chapa, Ethiopia's licensed payment gateway, and confirmed server-side before your listing is upgraded — nothing is marked Featured until the payment actually clears. This is separate from our optional escrow service, which protects a buyer's payment for the vehicle itself rather than upgrading a listing's visibility.

Want a different way to stand out? Getting your seller account verified is free and adds trust signal to every one of your listings, Featured or not.
