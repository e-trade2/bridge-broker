Commission on sale

Beyond the optional Featured upgrade, our only other fee is a commission — a percentage of the final sale price — charged only when your vehicle actually sells. There's no fixed published rate: an admin sets the rate for your specific listing and tells you before it's approved and published, so you always know the number before you agree to list.

Most sales close with a normal in-person handoff, and we collect the commission separately once the sale is confirmed. If you use our optional escrow protection instead, the commission is deducted automatically from the payout — one less thing to track.

Verified Broker Badge

Getting your seller account verified is free — see the Verified Broker Badge page for what it involves and why it's worth doing.

Buyer protection (Escrow)

Optional, and only used when a buyer specifically asks for it. There's no separate platform fee for escrow itself — it uses the same commission already agreed for that listing, deducted automatically when funds are released.
