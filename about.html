About Us

Bridging buyers and sellers, the honest way

Ethiopia's vehicle marketplace for Cars, Motorcycles, and Bajajs.

Bridge Broker exists because buying or selling a vehicle in Ethiopia usually means relying on word of mouth, a stranger's phone number, and a fair amount of trust that things will work out. We wanted a simpler, more transparent way to do it — one place to post a listing, one place to browse what's available, and a broker in the middle who actually reviews every listing before it goes live.

What we do

We're a vehicle marketplace and brokerage. Sellers list their car, motorcycle, or bajaj with us; we review each submission before it's published, replace the seller's contact details with our own broker line so every inquiry comes through us, and help connect serious buyers with sellers. When a sale happens, we take a commission — agreed with the seller upfront, before the listing ever goes live, never as a surprise after the fact.

How we're different

  • Every listing is reviewed. Nothing goes live without an admin checking it first.
  • Verification is real, not decorative. A "✓ Verified" badge means an admin actually reviewed that seller's ID — sellers can never verify themselves.
  • Optional buyer protection. Most sales happen the normal way — an in-person handoff — but if a buyer wants extra security, we offer an optional escrow option where funds are held until the handoff is confirmed.
  • No hidden fees. Our commission rate is set per listing and communicated to the seller before approval, not buried in fine print.

We're a young, growing platform — not yet a registered company, and still building out our processes. We'll update this page as that changes. What won't change is reviewing every listing and being upfront about how we make money.

Who we serve

Anyone in Ethiopia buying or selling a car, motorcycle, or bajaj — whether it's brand new or locally used. We work with individual sellers and dealers alike, and buyers never need to register an account just to browse or call about a listing.

Have a question we haven't answered here? Get in touch — we read every message.
