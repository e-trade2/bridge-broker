Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Bridge Broker ("we," "us") operates this vehicle marketplace. This page explains what information we collect, why, and how it's protected. We're a growing platform and not yet a registered company — we'll update this page as our structure changes.

Information we collect

From sellers and buyers who register an account

  • Email address and password (used for login — we never see your raw password, it's handled by our authentication provider)
  • Display name and phone number, if you set up a seller profile

From anyone who submits a listing

  • Vehicle details, photos, price, and your contact phone number (replaced with our broker number once the listing is approved and published — your original number is not shown to buyers)

If you request seller verification

  • Your full legal name and phone number
  • A photo of your ID or business license, used solely to confirm your identity

From buyers who use escrow (optional)

  • Name, phone number, and email — collected directly by the admin who creates the escrow link, since buyers don't need an account to use this feature

If you use Delala AI, our chat assistant

  • The messages you type are sent to our AI provider to generate a response (see "Third parties" below)

How we use your information

  • To review and publish your listing
  • To connect buyers and sellers (via our broker number, not your personal one)
  • To verify seller identity, when you request it
  • To process optional Featured Listing payments and optional escrow transactions
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or other third parties.

Who can see what

  • Public: Approved listings (vehicle details, photos, price, our broker phone/Telegram), and whether a seller is Verified.
  • Admins only: Pending/rejected listings, your original contact phone number before it's replaced, verification documents, and escrow/payment records.
  • You: Your own listings (including pending ones), your own verification request status, and any escrow transaction tied to a listing you sold.

Verification ID documents are stored privately and are never publicly accessible — only you and reviewing admins can view them, enforced at the database level, not just hidden in the interface.

Third parties we work with

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Chapa — Ethiopia's licensed payment gateway, processes Featured Listing and escrow payments. We never see or store your card or mobile money credentials directly; Chapa handles that.
  • Groq — powers Delala AI, our chat assistant. Messages you send it are processed by Groq's API to generate a response.

Stored in your browser, not on our servers

  • Your Delala AI chat history is saved in your browser's local storage so it's still there if you reopen the page. We don't store it on our servers, and it stays only on your device until you clear it (use the chat's clear-history option, or clear your browser data).
  • Listings you tap ❤️ to save are kept the same way — in your browser only, tied to this device, not your account.

Data retention

We keep account and listing data for as long as your account is active. If you'd like your data deleted, contact us (see below) and we'll process the request, subject to records we're required to keep for completed payments or escrow transactions.

Your choices

  • You can edit your own profile details (display name, phone) at any time from your Seller Dashboard.
  • You can request account or data deletion by contacting us.
  • You are never required to create an account just to browse listings or call about one.

This is a working draft, not reviewed by a lawyer. If you have specific privacy concerns, please contact us directly.
