Verified Broker Badge

A real trust signal — not something you can switch on yourself.

The ✓ Verified badge appears next to a seller's name on every one of their listings once an admin has personally reviewed and confirmed their identity. It tells buyers: this seller is who they say they are.

Why it matters

Buyers are naturally cautious about big purchases from strangers. A Verified badge cuts through that — it signals you've gone through an extra step most casual sellers skip, which tends to mean faster responses from serious buyers and fewer "is this real?" questions.

How verification works

  1. Submit a request. From your Seller Dashboard, go to "Get Verified," enter your full name and phone number, and upload a photo of your ID or business license.
  2. An admin reviews it. Your document is stored privately and only visible to you and the reviewing admin — never shown publicly. We check that it's a real, valid ID matching the details you submitted.
  3. You're notified of the outcome. If approved, the ✓ Verified badge appears immediately on all your current and future listings. If rejected, you can review the reason and submit again.

This can't be faked. Verified status is only ever set by an admin — there's no setting a seller can flip themselves, even by editing their own profile. That's by design: the badge only means something if sellers can't grant it to themselves.

Is it free?

Yes — getting verified costs nothing. We review submissions because it protects buyers and serious sellers alike, not as a revenue source.

What happens to my ID document?

Your uploaded document is kept in private storage, accessible only to you and admins reviewing verification requests — it's never displayed publicly or attached to your listings. See our Privacy Policy for more on how we handle this.

Ready to get verified? Head to your Seller Dashboard and look for "Get Verified."
